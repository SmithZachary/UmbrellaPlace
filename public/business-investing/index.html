What We Look For

Our Investment Criteria

We back businesses and teams that check these boxes.

Strong Founding Teams

Experienced, resilient operators with deep domain expertise and a bias toward execution.

Clear Market Opportunity

A large or fast-growing addressable market with validated demand and identifiable customer segments.

Scalable Business Model

Unit economics that improve with scale and a clear path from early traction to sustainable growth.

Capital Efficiency

Disciplined use of capital with a clear understanding of burn rate, runway, and path to profitability.

Defensible Advantage

Proprietary technology, network effects, or unique positioning that creates a durable competitive moat.

Alignment on Vision

Shared values around transparency, long-term thinking, and building something that matters.
