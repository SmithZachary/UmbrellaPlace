Our Story

Two brothers who served their country, built businesses, and now help others do the same.

Cole Smith

Managing Partner

Cole served as a human intelligence collector and Arabic linguist before commissioning as an officer in the U.S. Army. He continued as a Special Agent deploying to countries in crisis. After government service, he founded a SaaS company and now advises and invests in early-stage businesses. His drive to connect capital with opportunity led him to start Umbrella Place with his brother Zach.

Zachary Smith

Managing Partner

Zach flew as a Navy pilot before transitioning into software engineering, earning a Master’s degree and building a career solving complex technical problems. His ability to analyze systems and cut through complexity pulled him into real estate capital brokerage—investors move fast and need financing that keeps pace. He lives on the Florida Gulf Coast with his wife and four kids.
